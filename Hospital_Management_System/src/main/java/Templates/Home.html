Hospital Management System

A Hospital Management System (HMS) is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline healthcare operations and improve patient care. The system efficiently manages patient registration, appointment scheduling, doctor consultations, medical history, prescriptions, laboratory reports, billing, and hospital administration. Doctors can access patient records, update treatment details, and generate prescriptions, while patients can view their medical history, reports, and appointments online. The HMS reduces paperwork, enhances accuracy, improves communication between departments, and ensures better healthcare service management.
